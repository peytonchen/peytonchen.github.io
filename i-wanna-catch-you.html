JUMP !

The beginning of my story

 

 

There had been one of my friend tell me I looked like jumping when I walk. Since then, I think “jump” might be the representation of myself. That is the reason why I created the character. It emphasize my personality.

 

First, this character had made alive in real world. I used cloth, steel strips and hula hoop to finished it. I wear it on my body and dance to the music. After the experience, I can really get to know this character. Where does it live?  How can it react? What is the story of it? These kind of question kept come up with. “I MUST tell this story. ” the idea came to my mind.

 

- Storyboard -

How  to make the story alive?

 

I use pen to draw and inspired my character. Created the scene and made my characters acting, deforming in Maya. After that I cut the film, add all sound effect and music in Premiere.
